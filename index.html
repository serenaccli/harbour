What Harbour does

One system. Three signals.

Live marine risk map

Harbour scans the launch area and colors nearby sectors green, amber, or red using live swell, wind, and pressure data.

Fatigue-aware recommendation

Sleep and time-at-sea inputs shift the final call, so the same weather can produce a different recommendation.

Break-even reality check

Trip economics are folded into the reasoning, exposing when financial pressure is pushing unsafe decisions.
